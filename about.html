About Thunder Ridge Ampride

Fueling Iowa with Heart Since 2002

Our Story

In 2002, Thunder Ridge Ampride began as a single local stop for fuel and everyday essentials. Built on respect, hard work, and a strong connection to the community, we’ve grown — not just in size, but in spirit.

Today, we’re proud to operate five unique locations across Cedar Falls and Waterloo. Though each store has its own name and flavor, they all share the same commitment: to serve with kindness, keep things honest, and be a place our neighbors can count on — day or night.

Why We Do What We Do

We know that what we offer is more than just gas or groceries — it’s convenience in your busy life, a friendly face during your commute, and a safe stop on the road home.

Every customer matters to us. Whether you’re fueling up on your way to work, grabbing snacks for the road, or just popping in for a chat — you’re not just a visitor. You’re part of the community we serve and love.

Our Promise

  • ✅ Treat every person with dignity and respect
  • ✅ Keep our stores clean, safe, and welcoming
  • ✅ Support local jobs and local pride
  • ✅ Show up every day — rain or shine

We’re more than a gas station. We’re a reflection of the people who walk through our doors. And after 20+ years, we’re just getting started.
